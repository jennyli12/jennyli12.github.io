A Single Fibre Computer Enables Textile Networks and Distributed Inference

June 2023 - August 2023

C ▪ Python ▪ embedded ▪ Bluetooth Low Energy

Research project with fibers@mit, a group focusing on thermally-drawn fibers with embedded electronics that can be woven into clothing. Each fiber computer consists of an MCU (MAX32660), sensors (I2C), and a BLE SoC (DA14531); together, they form a multi-peripheral BLE network capable of monitoring biometric data and producing inference output (exercise pattern recognition, heart rate monitoring, etc.). Currently being reviewed for publication.
